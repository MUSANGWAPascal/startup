About

We Are the Best in taking you abroad

SALTEL ABROAD CONNECTION (SABROCON) is a Rwanda based educational consulting and recruitment agency with a worldwide reach. We assist people inside Rwanda and outside widely from East Africa to go abroad through STUDY ABROAD and/or WORK ABROAD PROGRAM. Since 2011, we have been offering these services, and we currently collaborate respectively with significant universities and businesses across Europe, North America, Asia, and other continents.
