About Us

SALTEL ABROAD CONNECTION(SABROCON)

SABROCON

SALTEL ABROAD CONNECTION (SABROCON) is a Rwanda based educational consulting and recruitment agency with a worldwide reach. We assist people inside Rwanda and outside widely from East Africa to go abroad through STUDY ABROAD and/or WORK ABROAD PROGRAM. Since 2011, we have been offering these services, and we currently collaborate respectively with significant universities and businesses across Europe, North America, Asia, and other continents.

On the side of STUDY ABROAD, we provide international students with all services related to the study abroad journey. We help students pursue their goal of an international education by providing guidance and counselling. We help students comprehend the complexities of the admissions process, application procedures, and range of study options available internationally.

On the side of WORK ABROAD, we assist people in locating employment outside of Rwanda. We have qualified and trusted advisors on foreign staffing.

Mission, Vision and Values

Mission, Vision and Values

Mission
  • To understand the needs and preferences of our clients so we stay focused on our core values to consistently provide exceptional services.
  • To facilitate students who seek to study abroad so they can equip themselves with skills and wisdom to give back to their respective community.
  • To make it regularly easy for people who dream of working abroad
Vision
  • With knowledge, respect, integrity and professionalism, to become a preferred and reliable supplying agency for international educational institutions and employment.
  • To be a solution providing consultancy for the international students seeking to study abroad.
Values
  • Trust: As a top-notch, we are high committed to providing employment and human resource services, we listen to our clients actively, build trust through clarity – a trusted partner and resource for our customers.
  • Authenticity: We deliver services with our honesty towards our customers and we take responsibility for the sake of our customers’ satisfaction.
  • Respect: In every interaction, we treat every individual with dignity.
  • Integrity: We conduct business with transparency and trust, adhering to the strictest ethical guidelines set forth by our sector; honesty, trustworthy, responsible, and caring.
  • Professionalism: We are reliable, accountable, and good communicators as we work under ethics for the sake of every stakeholder. We always learn new things and getting ready for new challenges to provide everyone with good quality service.
